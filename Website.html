Opened by two good friends, Chu Chu's Chow is a staple here in Dallas. After a fire burned it down in early 2005 our community rallied and helped rebuild the restaurant. We dedicate ourselves to superior service and we love giving back to our community. Come try our American classics, fill up on some cold root beer, and maybe stay for karaoke night!
